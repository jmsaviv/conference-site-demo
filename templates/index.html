Chappelle's Show Reunion Conference

Chappelle's Show was a massively popular TV show hosted by comedian Dave Chappelle from 2003 to 2006. The show abruptly ended when Dave Chappelle unexpectedly quit in 2006, much to the dismay of his fans. Fortunately for comedy fanatics everywhere, Dave has organized this conference as a sort of reunion for cast members of Chappelle's Show. The conference is open to the public and will feature several of the show's stars including Dave Chappelle himself.

July 22nd and 23rd, 2017
